Why GhosTrix?

At Ghostrix, we make entering the world of computer science easier for first-year students and those switching from other fields. Our mission is to provide clear guides, resources, and roadmaps for aspiring frontend developers, backend engineers, data scientists, and data analysts. With curated learning paths and a supportive community, Ghostrix helps you stay on track and confident in your journey, whether you're just starting or exploring a new area. We're here to guide your growth.
